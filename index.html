People feel losses 2x more intensely than equivalent gains. Our system detects loss-aversion-driven decisions and reframes choices to reduce emotional bias.

Financial health is driven by behavior, not just income. Our Well-being Index measures perceived financial security, capacity, and freedom as behavioral outputs.
